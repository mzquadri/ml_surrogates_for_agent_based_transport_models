   Impact on UQ quality with full dataset is unknown.

2. **Ensemble evaluation mismatch**: Exp A and B near-zero R² (~0.003) is a data
   distribution mismatch artifact, NOT individual model failure.
   T8 standalone R²=0.5957 is unaffected.

3. **sigma not calibrated**: k95=11.65. Use sigma for ranking, not probability intervals.

4. **T1 not comparable to T2-T8**: T1 uses Linear(64->1) final layer.
   T1's R²=0.7860 cannot be directly compared to T8's R²=0.5957.

5. **Single city (Paris)**: Generalization to other networks not validated.

6. **Selective prediction context**: 39.9% improvement = 50% retention on 50 eval graphs
   (not 100 graphs, not the full test set).

7. **65.8% conformal efficiency gain**: Applies to low-sigma (Q1) subset predictions only,
   not to the full test set.
